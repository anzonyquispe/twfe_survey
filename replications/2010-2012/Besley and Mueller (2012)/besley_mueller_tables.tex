\section*{3. Conclusion}

The dCDH web appendix classifies this paper as a sharp design 
where the stable groups assumption is \textit{not} satisfied. 
The treatment (killings) varies continuously across regions and time, 
meaning all groups are potentially  at different intensities. 
The weight decomposition reveals whether this heterogeneity 
leads to negative weights in the TWFE estimator.
