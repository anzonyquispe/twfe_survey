*------ 1991-2008 portion (D1) ------*

D1_MM_minwage.do organizes the programs (the D1 series of programs) that create the 
1991-2008 portion of the data from scratch using the raw CEX data and dictionary files.

*------ 1982-1990 portion (D2) ------*

D2_MM_pull_pre91.do organizes the programs that format the 1982-1990 portion of the data.
Rather than creating the programs from scratch, these programs used a pre-existing CEX
dataset that was created in house at the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago.

*------ Other notes (D3) ------*

D3_MM_add_heqdetail.do organizes a set of programs that bring in additional household 
equity variables that were not included in the original programs.

*------ Bringing it all together ------*

collage_aug10_update.do combines all of the datasets to create the data that is used 
by cex.do: ces_int_82_08.dta.
